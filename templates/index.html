About Mail Interview?

Mail Intervie is a simple software for make interviews in mailing lists.

What does it do?

  • Surveys of its customers: Create simple surveys to study the views of your customers and improve business.

How to Use?

Create a poll and get the generated html-code that you can send to all its customers. Voting will take place by moving customers to a specially crafted link. After the closure of the voting people can see the results, and you will receive HTML-code for publication or distribution to users.
